# Global AI Attitudes: Cultural, Technical, and Educational Perspectives

[![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.8+-blue.svg)](https://www.python.org/)
[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green.svg)](LICENSE)

**Interdisciplinary analysis of Pew Research Center's 2025 Global Attitudes Survey on AI awareness, concern, and trust across 23 countries (N=25,270).**

## Research Questions

1. How does education and internet access predict AI awareness globally?
2. Why are lower-income regions more excited about AI than high-income countries?
3. Does greater AI awareness increase or decrease trust in national regulation?

## Interdisciplinary Framing

- **Anthropology**: Cultural meanings of technology shape optimism vs. concern
- **Computer Science**: System opacity affects public confidence in AI governance  
- **Education**: AI literacy gaps create new digital divides
